Our Story

Built on 14 Years of
Engineering Trust

Aakar Fibre Glass was founded in 2011 in Delhi with a simple mission: to manufacture premium-quality FRP fiberglass products that Indian industries could depend on. What started as a small manufacturing operation has grown into one of Delhi's most trusted fiberglass solution providers.

Today, under the leadership of Mr. Ajay Sharma (Proprietor), we operate a fully-equipped manufacturing facility and modern warehouse in Delhi, serving contractors, developers, and industrial clients across India.

Our portfolio spans 50+ products across FRP roofing sheets, fiberglass domes, polyester resins, plain sheets, colour-coated roofing, and professional waterproofing services — all manufactured with stringent quality control and delivered on time.
